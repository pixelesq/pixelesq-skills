# Content Quality Rubric

Use this 10-point rubric to evaluate content before publishing. Each item scores 0 (missing), 1 (needs work), or 2 (strong). Target: 16+ out of 20.

## 1. Headline Clarity

- Does the headline communicate the page's value in under 12 words?
- Is it specific (numbers, outcomes) rather than vague?
- Would someone understand what the page offers from the headline alone?

**Strong:** "Build Landing Pages 3x Faster with AI-Powered Sections"
**Weak:** "Welcome to Our Amazing Platform"

## 2. Value Specificity

- Does the content state concrete benefits, not just features?
- Are outcomes quantified where possible?
- Is the "so what?" answered for every claim?

**Strong:** "Reduce page load time to under 2 seconds" (benefit + metric)
**Weak:** "Fast performance" (vague feature)

## 3. CTA Strength

- Does every page have at least one clear call to action?
- Do CTA labels use action verbs (Get, Start, Build, Join)?
- Is there a supporting line reducing friction ("Free trial, no credit card")?
- Is the CTA above the fold and repeated at page end?

## 4. Brand Voice Match

- Does the tone match the project's `contentGeneration.tone`?
- Is formality consistent with `contentGeneration.formality`?
- Does vocabulary match the `contentGeneration.readingLevel`?
- Does the personality feel like the brand throughout?

## 5. Keyword Integration

- Is the primary keyword in the title and first paragraph?
- Are secondary keywords used naturally (not stuffed)?
- Does the content answer the search intent behind the target keywords?
- Are keywords in headings where appropriate?

## 6. Readability

- Are paragraphs short (2-4 sentences max)?
- Are lists used for 3+ related items?
- Do headings create a scannable hierarchy (H1 > H2 > H3)?
- Is there adequate whitespace between sections?
- Is the reading level appropriate for the target audience?

## 7. Internal Links

- Are there links to at least 2-3 related pages or entries?
- Is anchor text descriptive (not "click here" or "read more")?
- Do links feel natural within the content flow?
- Are new pages linked from existing relevant content?

## 8. Media Usage

- Does each major section have a relevant image or visual?
- Are images sourced from project assets (`list_assets`)?
- Do images have alt text describing their content?
- Is there visual variety (not the same stock photo style throughout)?

## 9. Accessibility

- Do images have meaningful alt text?
- Is there sufficient heading hierarchy for screen readers?
- Are link texts descriptive enough to understand out of context?
- Is color not the only way information is conveyed?

## 10. Uniqueness

- Does the content provide information not easily found elsewhere?
- Is there a unique angle, data point, or perspective?
- Does it go beyond surface-level treatment of the topic?
- Would this content be worth bookmarking or sharing?

---

## Scoring Guide

- **18-20**: Publish-ready. Excellent quality.
- **16-17**: Good. Minor improvements optional.
- **12-15**: Needs revision. Address weak areas before publishing.
- **Below 12**: Significant rework needed. Review brand voice, structure, and value proposition.

## Quick Application

When reviewing content:

1. Score each dimension 0-2
2. Flag any dimension scoring 0 (critical gaps)
3. Prioritize fixes by impact: headlines and CTAs affect conversion most, readability and links affect SEO most
4. Re-score after revisions
