## Single-Font Strategies

Using one font for both headings and body creates a unified, minimal look. It works well when:
- The brand is minimal/clean
- The font has good weight variety (light through bold)
- You want consistency over contrast

**Best single-font choices:**
- **Inter** -- versatile at every weight, extremely readable
- **DM Sans** -- clean geometric with personality
- **Poppins** -- friendly, works at all sizes
- **Source Sans Pro** -- professional, neutral
- **Montserrat** -- bold headings, clean body at lighter weights
- **Rubik** -- modern, slightly rounded character
